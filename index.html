Hybrid

Barista FIRE.

Build a smaller portfolio, then bridge the rest with part-time work — often for the healthcare benefits alone. Common at Starbucks, Costco, and UPS (benefits at 20–25 hrs/week).

Formula

(Annual Spend − Part-Time Income) × 25

Employer coverage typically saves $6k–$18k/yr for a household.

Compounding magic

Coast FIRE.

Save enough early that compound growth alone carries you to a full FIRE number by your traditional retirement age. After Coast, you can stop saving — though you still need to cover your living expenses.

Formula

Coast # = FIRE Number ÷ (1 + r)^(retirement age − current age)
